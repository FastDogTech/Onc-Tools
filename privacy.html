Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

FastDog Tech is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by our Onc Tools mobile application.

Data Collection

Onc Tools is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or patient data. All calculations and operations are performed locally on your device.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No patient data is stored or transmitted
  • No location data is collected
  • No usage analytics are tracked

App Functionality

Our app provides calculation tools and reference information for oncology nurses. All computations are performed locally on your device, and no data is saved or transmitted to external servers.

Data Security

Since all calculations are performed locally and no data is stored or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach or unauthorized access to sensitive information.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs that would collect or process your information.

Medical Disclaimer

Onc Tools is designed as a calculation and reference aid for healthcare professionals. All calculations should be independently verified before clinical use. This app is not intended to replace clinical judgment, professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

The app provides calculations based on established medical formulas. Users are responsible for verifying the accuracy of all calculations and their appropriateness for specific clinical situations.

Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for use by healthcare professionals and does not collect any information from any person, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and in the app.

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of March 4, 2026, and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future.
