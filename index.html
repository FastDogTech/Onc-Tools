For Healthcare Professionals

Onc Tools is designed exclusively for oncology nurses, pharmacists, and healthcare providers working in oncology settings. All calculations follow established clinical formulas and guidelines.

Key Features

BSA Calculator

Calculate Body Surface Area using Dubois or Mosteller formulas with built-in calculator for medication amounts.

AUC Calculator

Calculate chemotherapy amounts based on Area Under Curve using Calvert formula with creatinine clearance.

Weight Calculator

Calculate using actual, ideal, or adjusted body weight with comprehensive validation.

GFR & Creatinine Clearance

Calculate kidney function using Cockcroft-Gault and MDRD equations for accurate renal assessment.

ANC Calculator

Calculate Absolute Neutrophil Count quickly to assess infection risk and treatment timing.

Date Finder

Calculate chemotherapy administration dates with precision, including past and future cycles.

Medical Disclaimer: Onc Tools is designed as a reference and calculation aid for healthcare professionals. All calculations should be independently verified before clinical use. This app does not provide medical advice and is not intended to replace clinical judgment.
